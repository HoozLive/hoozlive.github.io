Grounded is a first and third-person cooperative survival game by Obsidian Entertainment and Xbox Game Studios. It was first revealed at X019 in London and entered early access on July 28th, 2020.
Grounded became available through early access July 28th 2020 for Xbox One and Windows digitally via Game Preview with Xbox Game Pass. Updated through user feedback during development, it is the first Game Preview title available through Microsoft's Xbox Game Pass. Grounded is also available through Steam Early Access. It is unknown when it will fully release.
